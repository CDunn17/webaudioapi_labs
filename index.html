A set of browser-based workbenches for building sound effects, music, and voice-inspired audio. Every result is procedural: you can listen, adjust the underlying controls, and reuse the configuration without shipping a recorded sample.

How this works

From an idea to editable audio

Pick the workbench that matches your starting point. Nothing is permanent, so the best way to learn is to play a sound and make small changes as you listen.

  1. 01
    Choose a starting point

    Begin with a preset, build a score, or record a sound with your voice.

  2. 02
    Listen and shape

    Preview often, adjust the controls, and compare the result with what you heard.

  3. 03
    Keep the recipe

    Copy or save the generated config when it is ready to use in another project.

Before you begin

A few tips for better results

  • Start quietly. Lower your device volume before the first preview.
  • Change one thing at a time. Small moves make each control easier to understand.
  • Record cleanly. In Voice Lab, use a quiet room and leave clear space between sounds.
  • Save good iterations. Copy or download a config before exploring a new direction.
